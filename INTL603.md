***

Students examines marketing practices in a dynamic global environment. In the module, students will examine decisions that marketing managers make and begin the process of decision-making using research and marketing tools, both quantitative and qualitative research methods, and cultural understanding when expanding into a new market. The module assumes familiarity with basic marketing concepts and utilizes this as a base to develop insights into global environments.hyjgj
